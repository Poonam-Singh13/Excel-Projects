Coffee Sales Dashboard -
Overview
This interactive dashboard provides a comprehensive visualization of coffee sales data across multiple dimensions including time, geography, customer behavior, and product attributes. It is designed to help stakeholders analyze trends, identify top-performing regions and customers, and make data-driven decisions.
Key Features
- Order Date Filter: Select sales data from July 2021 to December 2022.
- Total Sales Over Time: Line chart showing monthly sales (USD) for four coffee types—Arabica, Excelsa, Liberica, and Robusta—from January 2019 to July 2022.
- Sales by Country: Bar chart highlighting top-performing countries:
- United States: $35,638.89
- Ireland: $6,696.87
- United Kingdom: $2,798.51
- Top 5 Customers: Bar chart showcasing highest individual spenders:
- Allis Wilmore: $317.07
- Brenn Dundredege: $307.05
- Terri Farra: $289.11
- Nealson Cutler: $281.68

- Interactive Filters:
- Size: 0.2, 0.5, 1, 2.5
- Roast Type: Dark, Light, Medium
- Loyalty Card Status: Yes / No
