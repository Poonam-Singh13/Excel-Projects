Sales Analysis Dashboard Documentation
--------------------------------------

Overview:
The "Sales Analysis" dashboard provides a comprehensive view of sales performance across multiple dimensions. It is designed to support data-driven decision-making by presenting key performance indicators (KPIs), revenue trends, and order distribution patterns in a clear and interactive format.

Key Metrics:
- Total Orders: 1,000
- Total Revenue: ₹35,20,984
- Correlation between Order Date & Delivery Date: 5.35
- Average Revenue: ₹3,205

Visualizations:
1. Revenue by Occasion:
   Displays revenue contributions across occasions such as Anniversary, Birthday, Diwali, Holi, Raksha Bandhan, and Valentine’s Day.

2. Revenue by Category:
   Breaks down revenue by product categories including Cakes, Colors, Mugs, Plants, Soft Toys, Raksha Bandhan items, and Sweets.

3. Revenue by Month:
   Shows monthly revenue trends from January to December, highlighting seasonal variations.

4. Revenue by Top 5 Products:
   Identifies the highest revenue-generating products (e.g., Deserunt Box, Dolores Gift, Harum Pack, Magnum Set, Quia Gift).

5. City by Order Count:
   Highlights order distribution across cities such as North Dumdum, Kavali, Imphal, Haridwar, Guntakal, Dibrugarh, Dhanbad, and Bilaspur.

6. Revenue by Hour:
   Provides insights into hourly revenue patterns, useful for understanding peak sales times.

Filters:
- Order Date: Selectable by month (e.g., June–September 2023).
- Delivery Date: Selectable by month (e.g., June–September 2024).
- Occasion: Filterable by specific events (Anniversary, Birthday, Diwali, Holi, etc.).

Purpose:
This dashboard enables stakeholders to:
- Monitor overall sales performance.
- Identify high-performing occasions, categories, and products.
- Detect seasonal and hourly sales trends.
- Assess geographic distribution of orders.
- Optimize marketing and inventory strategies based on data insights.

Conclusion:
The Sales Analysis dashboard is a powerful tool for visualizing and interpreting sales data. By combining KPIs with detailed breakdowns across multiple dimensions, it provides actionable insights that can guide business growth and operational efficiency.
